Importing Tokens

As for importing tokens, here are four steps listed for you.

Step1. Asset deployment

  • Deploy mapping contracts of the corresponding asset on the target chain using the template.
  • Ensure the total amount of contracts stays consistent with the amount on the source chain.

Step2. Assets transfer

  • Transfer the initialized assets to the target chain proxy contract.

Step3. Information submission

  • Download the asset template and fill in the information according to an example in the template.
  • Submit the .xlsx file to poly.
  • Contact Poly Network via

Step4. Test

  • Wait until the operators successfully add your contract address to the whitelist.
  • Click here and follow the User Manual to test the token cross-chain transfer.
